Stakeholders:

1: General Manager: 
   Requests candidates from the system.

2: HR Manager: 
   Initiates and oversees the recruitment process, schedules interviews, manages candidate data, and facilitates onboarding.

3: Interviewers:
   Conduct interviews, provide feedback, and confirm interview schedules through the app.

4: Candidates: 
   While they do not interact directly with the system for interview scheduling, feedback, offer acceptance/decline, or onboarding details,they are a critical part of the process(These activities are managed through other means.).

5: System Admin: Manage technical aspects of user access.
